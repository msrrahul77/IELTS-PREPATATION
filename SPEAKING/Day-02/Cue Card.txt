PART 1 
Let’s talk about your hometown.
Where is your hometown located?
Could you tell me about your hometown?
What do you like most about living there?
Where should a tourist visit in your hometown?
How has your hometown changed over the years?
If you could change one aspect of your hometown, what would it be?

PART 2
Talk about a study strategy that you have used to improve your learning?
You should say:
What is this learning methodology?
When and why did you start doing this?
How has this study practice impacted your learning and school performance?
Can you improve this method in any way?
You will have one to two minutes to talk about this topic.
You will have one minute to prepare what you are going to say.
